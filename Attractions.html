    4. Definitions for the images above

    Koko
    A Hawaiian word meaning blood or red earth.
    Shark
    A large, long-bodied marine fish with a cartilaginous skeleton, a prominent dorsal fin, and toothlike scales.
    Most sharks are predatory, although the largest kinds feed on plankton, and some can grow to a very large size.
    Byodo
    A Japanese word meaning Temple of Equality or Equal.
